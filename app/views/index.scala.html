Our Story

What is your favorite thing about Kelly?

I love how Kelly is always so happy. And what makes her happy is very simple. She can be happy because she learned how to make delicious red bean hand-knead bread. She can also be happy because she gets to eat a Dick’s Deluxe burger & soggy fries for late night snack. It’s the little joys in life that she looks forward to and I have been trying my best to keep her happy and allow her to do the things she wants. That’s why I am now a Grand-Master League in dish washing & clean-up ;D.

Describe your most memorable date.

I don't remember an official "date". But my most memorable moment about us was when I asked her to be my girlfriend. Before I asked her, my heart was pounding so hard that even she could hear my heartbeat. I am glad I was able to ask her before any cardiac arrest. Afterwards, we shared our feelings for each other for the past two years…..yes it took me that long to ask her… That was the beginning of our official love relationship!

How did the two of you love birds meet?

I met Kelly in a Math class at Ingraham High School. The first thing I noticed about Kelly was that she was quite smart and easygoing. (She later claimed that she scored higher than me on some tests in that class, but I don’t recall that part @@-@@.) I did not speak much English back then, but we still chatted on MSN Messenger using both Chinese and English. Even though we came from different backgrounds, we both had very laid-back personalities which made us very good friends and a good match for each other.

- Aron's Story
